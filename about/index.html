Our vision

A sanctuary of worship, a neighbour of peace.

The Virgin Mary Mosque exists to serve the Muslim community of Melbourne in the five daily prayers, Jumu’ah, the lunar festivals, and all the life-events in between — from the adhan whispered in a newborn’s ear to the Janazah prayer that accompanies us home.

We also exist as a neighbour. Because our building is named for Maryam — honoured by Muslims and Christians alike — we carry a particular responsibility: to build dialogue, to welcome visitors, and to make the mosque a place where people of other faiths feel safe asking questions.
