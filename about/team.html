Imam

Shaykh ‘Abdullah al-Maryami

Our Imam trained at Al-Azhar in Cairo and served congregations in Jordan and Sydney before joining the Virgin Mary Mosque. He leads daily prayers, the Friday khutbah, and our counselling programme.

Areas of focus: Qur’anic recitation and tafsir, interfaith engagement, and pastoral care.
