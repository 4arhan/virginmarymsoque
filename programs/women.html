Women’s programs

Led by and for the women of our congregation, in the tradition of a mosque named for Maryam (peace be upon her).

Weekly halaqa

Wednesdays 10:00 AM – 11:30 AM. Qur’an reflection, community tea and informal conversation.

Wellbeing circle

Fortnightly Sundays. A safe space to share, led by our female counsellor in confidence.

Mothers’ group

Mondays 10:00 AM. Babies and toddlers welcome. Activities and playgroup, plus peer support.

Book club

Monthly. Recent reads have included The Road to Mecca and Muhammad: His Life Based on the Earliest Sources.

Marriage & family

Quarterly workshops with the Imam and a family counsellor. See Nikah services.

Women’s prayer hall

A generous, visible prayer space for all daily prayers, Jumu’ah, Taraweeh and Eid. Wudu facilities adjacent.
