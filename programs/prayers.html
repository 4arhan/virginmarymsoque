Prayer times & Jumu’ah

Five daily prayers, Friday Jumu’ah, Eid, Taraweeh and the monthly Qiyam night.

Jumu’ah

  • First Jumu’ah: 12:30 PM (Arabic khutbah + short English summary)
  • Second Jumu’ah: 1:30 PM (English khutbah)
  • Duration: approx. 45 minutes including prayer
  • Parking: limited on-site, street parking on surrounding roads

Visiting for the first time? New and non-Muslim visitors are warmly welcomed. Our ushers will help you find the prayer hall and show the women’s entrance. What to expect →

Special prayers

Ramadan, Eid & Qiyam

Taraweeh

Nightly after Isha throughout Ramadan. Led by our huffaz; Qur’an is completed across the month.

Eid prayers

Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha prayers hosted on-site, with overflow marquee. Times announced a week prior.

Qiyam night

Last Saturday of every month (outside Ramadan) — tahajjud, short reminder, suhur.
