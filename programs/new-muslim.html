New-Muslim support

Exploring, considering, or ready to take your Shahada — you are welcome here, and you will not walk this road alone.

If you’re exploring

Start wherever you are comfortable. You are welcome to:

  • Visit the mosque for a prayer — our ushers will help you.
  • Attend a Sunday open session and ask questions, with no expectation to commit.
  • Browse our introduction to Islam in the Knowledge Hub.
  • Book a one-on-one conversation with the Imam (counselling page).

Taking your Shahada

When you are ready to declare belief, the Imam or a trusted member of the congregation will walk you through it. There is no fee, no paperwork, no public spectacle unless you want one. You simply say the words, in whatever language you are comfortable in, and we rejoice with you.

أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا رَسُولُ اللَّهِ “I bear witness that there is no god but Allah, and I bear witness that Muhammad is the messenger of Allah.”

After Shahada

Our new-Muslim programme includes:

  • Foundations class — six weekly sessions covering belief, prayer, purity, Ramadan and zakat.
  • Mentorship buddy — a member of your gender who has walked the road and will meet you weekly for the first six months.
  • Family support — we can help you navigate conversations with family of other faiths.
  • Library access — our reading list and audio lectures on a USB, lent to new Muslims for a year.
