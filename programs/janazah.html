Janazah (funeral) services

Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji’un. We are here to help — at any hour.

Immediate help: call our 24-hour Janazah line on +61 (0)3 0000 0000 (press 1 for funeral services). A volunteer will answer and walk you through the first steps.

What we coordinate

  • Ghusl (ritual washing) at a partner facility in Melbourne.
  • Kafan (shrouding) and dignified transport.
  • Janazah prayer at the mosque, usually after Dhuhr or Asr.
  • Burial at Fawkner, Bunurong, or another Muslim burial ground of your choice.
  • Paperwork — medical certificate, death registration, and coordination with funeral directors.

Costs

The mosque does not charge for Janazah prayers or pastoral support. Ghusl and burial costs are set by the relevant providers. If cost is a barrier, please speak to us — zakat and welfare funds are available to assist families in hardship.

After the burial

Our pastoral team will follow up in the days after the burial with condolence visits, meal support for the family, and an invitation to our grief circle (monthly, led by a bereavement-trained chaplain).
