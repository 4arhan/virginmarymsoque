Youth programs

Faith, friendship, fitness and futures — for teenagers and young adults across Melbourne.

Weekly

Youth halaqa (ages 13–18)

Every second Saturday, 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM. Short talk, group discussion, pizza. Led by our Imam and youth team.

Weekly

Young Adults Circle (18–30)

Thursdays 7:30 PM. Book discussions, Qur’an reflection, and roundtables on work, identity and faith.

Monthly

Sports & outdoor

Futsal, basketball, bushwalking and camp-outs. All gear provided; transport available for members.

Mentorship

Career & study

One-on-one mentoring from professionals in our congregation across medicine, engineering, law and trades.

Term breaks

School-holiday camps

Three-day camps in autumn and spring. Registration opens four weeks before each camp.

Safeguarding

Our commitments

All youth staff and volunteers hold a current Working with Children Check and complete annual safeguarding training.
