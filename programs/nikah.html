Nikah (marriage) services

A sound Nikah, conducted with care, by an Imam who has known you before the day itself.

What we provide

  • Pre-marital consultation — two sessions with the Imam covering the conditions of a sound Nikah, communication, and rights and responsibilities.
  • The Nikah ceremony — in the mosque, or at a venue of your choosing within Melbourne.
  • Civil registration — our Imam is a registered marriage celebrant under Australian law, so your Nikah is also your legal marriage.
  • Nikah certificate — in English and Arabic, signed by the Imam and witnesses.

Booking

Please give at least six weeks’ notice. The booking fee supports the mosque and covers document preparation. Concessions are available for members.

Enquire about a date

If either party is not Muslim, please speak to the Imam first. We work respectfully with mixed-faith couples and their families and will be transparent about what we can and cannot officiate.
