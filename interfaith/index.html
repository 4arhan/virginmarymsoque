Our posture

Not a fusion. A meeting.

Interfaith work at our mosque is not about blurring the distinctions between Islam and Christianity. It is about holding them honestly, in friendship. We disagree, and we say so; and we still share bread, attend each other’s weddings and funerals, and mourn and celebrate together.

If you are Christian and curious, or Jewish and curious, or of another tradition and curious — you are welcome. Come and ask us anything.
