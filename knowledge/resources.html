Privacy

The Virgin Mary Mosque is committed to handling your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles. We collect only the information we need to deliver our services, we do not sell your data, and you can ask for your information to be accessed, corrected or deleted at any time by emailing privacy@virginmarymosque.org.au.

Full privacy policy and terms are available on request from the mosque office.
