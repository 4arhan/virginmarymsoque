Named for Maryam (عليها السلام)

A mosque built around a shared story.

Maryam — Mary, mother of ‘Isa (Jesus) — is the only woman named in the Qur’an, and the 19th surah bears her name. Christians and Muslims revere her as a woman of extraordinary faith. Our mosque takes its name from hers as a standing invitation: come, share the reverence, and learn what our traditions hold in common.
