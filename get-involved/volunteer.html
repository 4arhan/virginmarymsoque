Volunteer with us

Our mosque runs on volunteers. Five hours a month can change what we are able to do.

Friday

Ushering & hospitality

Welcoming visitors, directing to prayer halls, tea service after Jumu’ah.

Year round

Teaching & tutoring

Saturday madrasah, adult classes, Arabic tutoring. Training and resources provided.

Ramadan + ongoing

Food hamper team

Sorting, packing and distributing hampers to families across Melbourne.

Weekends

Youth mentoring

Halaqas, camps, sports, and one-on-one mentoring. WWCC required.

Monthly

Interfaith team

Guided visits for school and church groups, event hosting, Q&A panels.

Skills-based

Skilled volunteering

Legal, accounting, design, IT, translation, safeguarding. A few hours a quarter.
