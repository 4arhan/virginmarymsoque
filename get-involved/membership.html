Become a member

Members shape the mosque’s direction, vote at the AGM, and receive priority bookings for classes, camps and Nikah services.

What members get

  • Voting rights at the Annual General Meeting
  • Priority bookings for classes, camps and the Nikah calendar
  • Discounted rates for events and short courses
  • Quarterly members’ newsletter (separate from the public one)
  • Invitations to members-only community dinners

What it costs

  • Individual membership: $60 / year
  • Family membership (same household): $100 / year
  • Student/concession: $20 / year

No one is turned away for cost. Speak to the office if the fee is a barrier.
